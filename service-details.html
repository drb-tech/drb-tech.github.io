Service Details

Fleet & Driver Monitoring

DRB’s advanced Fleet Monitoring system gives fleet managers real-time data on vehicle locations, fuel consumption, and route performance. This allows businesses to optimize fleet operations and reduce costs.

Additionally, the Driver Monitoring feature tracks behaviors such as speeding, harsh braking, and idling. It provides insights for driver training and safety improvements, helping to prevent accidents and reduce risk.

Data Analytics & Insights

DRB’s platform transforms raw data from vehicles into actionable insights. Fleet managers can view real-time reports on vehicle performance, driver behavior, and operational costs, enabling informed decision-making to enhance fleet efficiency.

Dynamic Routing & Optimization

DRB’s Dynamic Routing solution adjusts routes based on real-time traffic data, weather, and road conditions. By reducing idle time and improving route efficiency, businesses can save on fuel costs and improve on-time deliveries.

Telematics Hardware & Applications

Telematics Hardware

DRB’s telematics unit integrates OBD-II diagnostics, GPS, RFID passenger tracking, and driver monitoring cameras. It collects critical data, sending it to the cloud for real-time analysis, helping fleet managers stay informed about vehicle health and performance.

Human Interface Applications

Fleet managers, drivers, and passengers can access all relevant data through DRB’s user-friendly mobile and web applications. These apps provide real-time updates on vehicle locations, routes, and maintenance alerts, offering seamless control over fleet operations.
