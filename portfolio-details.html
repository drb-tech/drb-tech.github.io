Portfolio Details

Company Overview

DRB is a tech startup providing innovative software and hardware solutions in automotive, supply chain, and transportation industries. Focused on optimizing vehicle operations and safety, DRB’s mission is to deliver smarter transportation solutions for schools, taxis, ambulances, and other fleets.

Industry

DRB operates in the automotive and transportation sectors, specializing in fleet management. This technology-driven approach helps businesses track, monitor, and maintain vehicle fleets efficiently, ensuring safety and cost-effectiveness. DRB’s advanced solutions bring transparency and control to vehicle management.

Products and Services

DRB offers a suite of fleet management solutions tailored for organizations managing two or more vehicles. Our key offerings include:

Fleet Monitoring System

A flexible system offering driver behavior tracking, dynamic route planning, and real-time vehicle health insights.

Vehicle Monitoring

Real-time performance data, diagnostics, and maintenance alerts to help prevent breakdowns and maximize uptime.

Hardware Units

Embedded hardware featuring GPS, OBD-II, RFID, and driver monitoring systems, providing detailed vehicle and driver data.

Fleet Application

An intuitive app offering live updates, route tracking, and vehicle health reporting for fleet owners, drivers, and passengers.

Target Audience

DRB’s solutions are designed for organizations managing vehicle fleets such as schools, taxi companies, emergency services, rental car agencies, and logistics providers. Any business reliant on transportation can benefit from DRB’s technology.

Value Proposition

DRB’s fleet management system empowers organizations to enhance operational efficiency, boost safety, and gain actionable insights through advanced analytics. Customizable and modular, it adapts to specific fleet needs, offering real-time monitoring and data-driven decision-making tools.

Vision

DRB’s vision, embodied in the slogan “A SAFE WAY TO THEIR DREAMS”, reflects the company’s commitment to making transportation safer and more efficient. Whether for education, logistics, or emergency services, DRB is dedicated to helping organizations meet their goals safely and reliably.
